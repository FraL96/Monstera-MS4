About ORDERS & DELIVERY

Despite a lot of interest it is unfortunately not yet possible for us to ship outside Europe because the journey is too stressful for our plants and can cause them damage. Our team is working hard to find a way of packaging them safely to provide a solution for this, and as soon as we find a safe shipping method we will announce it on social media. Stay tuned!

Well you’ve come to the right place. We love plants (and nature), and our goal is to become 100% sustainable.
Unfortunately finding the right sustainable packaging is still a real challenge but we are constantly trying to improve.
All of our packaging is made from water bottles and other recycled materials.

Our plants are grown naturally in our greenhouse so depending on the season, the weather, the parent plant and the age of the plant, the actual plant you will receive might vary a bit from what you see in the picture on the online store.
In this way every plant is a unique object, and we wouldn’t have it any other way.
